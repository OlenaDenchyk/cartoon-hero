Amicia de Rune

About Amicia

The eldest child of the de Rune family, she and her brother are compelled to flee for their lives through the pestilent countryside of France when the Inquisition raids her family's château out of heresy and murders everybody to have her brother in their possession.

Amicia's Characteristics

  1. Born: Circa 1333 in Aquitaine, France
  2. Condition: Uninfected
  3. Religion: Catholic
  4. Rank: Aristocrat
  5. Weapon of choice: Sling
  6. Personality: Ready to take on the knight's challenge
